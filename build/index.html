Powerful Electric Motors

Easy Tow

Easy Tow is the world's first dual-motor Active Tow Assist System, designed for safer, effortless towing with extended range and improved efficiency. Compared to towing a traditional travel trailer of similar size and weight, gas-powered vehicles will experience a significant boost in MPG, while electric vehicles will travel much farther on a single charge.

Remote Control

Remote Control allows you to move around a campsite or driveway via the Pebble App, pivoting effortlessly, and positioning into tight spots with ease.

Magic Hitch

Magic Hitch allows one-touch sensing, self-positioning, and attaching to your tow vehicle.

Integrated Rooftop Solar

1.1 kW Solar Power

Four 290W roof-mounted solar panels, providing a constant power supply equivalent to being plugged into a 110V outlet. This system keeps your battery topped off, extending your off-grid adventures with ease and reliability

Auto Dump

The End of Dumping Dread

Auto Dump replaces the messy, complicated steps of traditional RV waste management with a streamlined, hygienic process. Equipped with a macerator pump, gray-water rinse, and integrated hookup, the system reduces water use and eliminates hassle. Tank levels and dumping are easily managed through the Pebble App.

All-terrain Levelers

Enhanced Leveling Capability

Working seamlessly with the four standard powered corner stabilizers and the front wheel jack, the powerful All-terrain Levelers deliver enhanced pitch and roll leveling capabilities for optimal stability and comfort on rough, uneven terrain. Fully app-enabled, you can easily level your Pebble Flow with just a tap.

Surround Vision

Security & Viewing
