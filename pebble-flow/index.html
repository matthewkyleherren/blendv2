FAQs

Do I need an EV to tow the Pebble Flow?

You don’t need an EV to tow The Pebble Flow.  Pebble Flow’s dual-motor Active Tow Assist System provides a safe and effortless towing experience and works great for gas, hybrid or EVs. As long as your vehicle has a towing capacity of at least 6,800 lbs, you’ll be able to tow the Pebble Flow.

How do I charge the Pebble Flow?

The Pebble Flow can be charged via  AC/DC charging at public stations, at home, campgrounds, and more. The Pebble Flow’s optional 1.1 kW integrated solar self-charges on the go and is always topping off its power supply.

Does the Pebble App require the Internet to connect and communicate with the Pebble Flow?

No. All communication necessary for Pebble Flow's functions is kept within a local network, bypassing the need for cloud or internet service providers. This localized communication enables Pebble Flow's features to operate seamlessly, regardless of your location, even in remote areas.

What's included in the Magic Pack

The Magic Pack adds a powerful dual-motor setup and bundles a set of world-first features to the Pebble Flow, including Remote Control, Magic Hitch, Easy Tow and more.

Can I power my home with the Pebble Flow?

Yes. The total battery capacity of the Pebble Flow is 3.5x that of a Tesla Powerwall, making it a great home backup battery in the case of an emergency.
