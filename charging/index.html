Charging Solutions

Charge via AC power at home or the campsite, utilize DC fast charging or regenerative charging while on the road, and harness solar energy wherever the sun shines.

Discover more

Real-time Energy Flow

Accessible through the Pebble App, the Energy View offers a seamless, real-time visualization of your power flow for a more integrated and convenient experience.

Universal Charging

The Pebble Flow delivers unparalleled energy flexibility, offering multiple ways to use and share power— at home, at the campsite, or on the go.

1.1 kW Solar Charging

The optional Integrated Rooftop Solar keeps you perpetually powered, like being continuously plugged into a 110V outlet. It enables self-charging while parked or on the move, keeping your 45 kWh LFP battery topped off effortlessly.

AC/DC Charging

With support for Level 1 and Level 2 AC charging, as well as Level 3 DC fast charging via the built-in CCS1 charge port, the Pebble Flow gives you the flexibility to charge wherever you need—at EV charging stations, at home, or at campgrounds.

Regenerative Braking

The Pebble Flow features regenerative braking, similar to electric vehicles, allowing it to recharge itself while being towed.

Export Power

Comes equipped with 12 kW of 240V AC output, so you can power your campsite equipment, appliances, your EV, another RV, or even your home.

Emergency Energy Backup

With a battery capacity 3.5x that of a Tesla Powerwall, the Pebble Flow makes a great home backup battery in case of emergencies.

Tech specs

  • 45 kWh LFP battery (Standard)
  • 1.1 kW Integrated Rooftop Solar (Optional)
  • AC Charging: Up to 6.6 kW (Standard)
  • DC Fast Charging: 0-100%: 1 hr (CCS1) (Standard)
  • Export Power: 12 kW (Standard)
