What's Pebble App?

The Pebble App serves as your command center and the gateway to all the intelligence integrated into the Pebble Flow. Everything you need is in the palm of your hand. From interior features like temperature control, dimmable lighting, and monitoring the level of your battery SoC and water tanks to high-tech controls like InstaCamp, Remote Control, Magic Hitch, and even checking your energy flow. The Pebble App is created to make RVing very simple and intuitive.

What's Pebble Flow's Limited Warranty?

  1. Pebble Flow: 3 years/30k miles
  2. Motors: 5 years/50k miles*
  3. Battery: 8 years/80k miles*
  4. Appliances, Fixtures & Accessories: 1 year/10k miles*

*whichever comes first

What is InstaCamp?

InstaCamp deploys or retracts stairs, lights, and auto-leveling stabilizers to set up camp or get back on the road in seconds with a push of a button.


What kind of battery is in the Pebble Flow?

The Pebble Flow is equipped with a 45 kWh LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) battery, which is the safest battery chemistry on the market.

What's the maximum speed for the Remote Control feature?

The maximum speed is set at walking speed.

What is Magic Hitch?

Magic Hitch allows Pebble Flow to position itself and automatically hitch onto the towing vehicle.

What is Remote Control?

Once unhooked from the hitch, the Pebble Flow is fully remote-controlled using the Pebble App for easy parking and maneuvering, effortlessly pivoting and positioning itself into tight spots.


Does the Pebble App require the Internet to connect and communicate with the Pebble Flow?

No. All communication necessary for Pebble Flow's functions is kept within a local network, bypassing the need for cloud or internet service providers. This localized communication enables Pebble Flow's features to operate seamlessly, regardless of your location, even in remote areas.

How do I charge the Pebble Flow

The Pebble Flow can be charged via AC/DC charging at public stations, at home, campgrounds, and more. The Pebble Flow’s optional 1.1 kW integrated rooftop solar self-charges on the go and is always topping off its power supply.

Do I need an EV to tow the Pebble Flow?

You don’t need an EV to tow The Pebble Flow. Pebble Flow’s dual-motor Active Tow Assist System provides a safe and effortless towing experience and works great for gas, hybrid or EVs. As long as your vehicle has a towing capacity of at least 6,800 lbs, you’ll be able to tow the Pebble Flow.

What's included in the Magic Pack?

The Magic Pack adds a powerful dual-motor setup and bundles a set of world-first features to the Pebble Flow, including Remote Control, Magic Hitch, Easy Tow and more.

Can I power my home with the Pebble Flow?

Yes. The total battery capacity of the Pebble Flow is 3.5x that of a Tesla Powerwall, making it a great home backup battery in the case of an emergency.

Does Easy Tow (Active Tow Assist System) lower the required towing capacity?

The required towing capacity is determined by the trailer’s Gross Vehicle Weight Rating (GVWR). Additionally, your tow vehicle must be capable of handling the tongue weight. Although the Pebble Flow's Active Tow Assist System significantly reduces the load on the tow vehicle when activated, the minimum towing capacity requirements remain unchanged (6,800 lbs) to ensure safety and compliance with regulations.

How long will the battery last on a single charge?

The 45 kWh battery, combined with the optional 1.1 kW of integrated rooftop solar, is enough to power all of the trailer's appliances and allow users to explore off-grid for up to seven days.

Is the Pebble Flow off-road capable?

While the Pebble Flow isn’t specifically designed as an off-road trailer, it’s built to handle a variety of terrains with ease. It features a durable caster wheel, 33” Goodyear all-season tires, four motorized stabilizers, and an optional set of all-terrain levelers for added capability. With a bumper ground clearance of 20 inches and a departure angle of 11 degrees, the Pebble Flow is ready to tackle most campsites.

What is the towing range?

The towing range is influenced by various factors, including the drag coefficient of the tow vehicle—both alone and when combined with the trailer—battery capacity, and the powertrain efficiency or fuel economy of your tow vehicle. The Pebble Flow’s active tow assist system and aerodynamic design are specifically engineered to optimize and maximize range and mileage, regardless of your tow vehicle. With Easy Tow activated, long-range EVs can achieve a towing range of up to 250 miles.

What is Easy Tow?

Easy Tow is the world’s first Dual-Motor Active Tow Assist System. The Pebble Flow has its own motors on board, resulting in effortless towing, maximized range, and unwavering confidence whether towing from a truck, large SUV, or EV. Thanks to this system, the Pebble Flow can also be towed with an EV without range anxiety.
